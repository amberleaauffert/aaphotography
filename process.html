THE PROCESS

~STEP ONE~

inquire

The first step in my process is to reach out to me! Tell me a little about yourself. Where and when you are wanting to capture your special moment and tell me the vibe you're going for!

~STEP TWO~

booking

After we decide what is best for you, I will mark you on my calendar (now it's getting serious)! I do require a non-refundable retainer to book your date, along with a signed contract. This protects both of us! We will also discuss your session after it's official.

~STEP THREE~

day-of

We will plan and be in touch leading up to your special day! I am always here to answer any questions and give any advice! You will have put a lot of work into planning and deserve to enjoy the day! Be prepared for an amazing experience. I am all yours for the day!
